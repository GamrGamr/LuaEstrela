What it does

Understand where your
electricity goes.

Enter the energy and contracted-power prices from your bill, then fill in whichever appliance values you know. Measured monthly kWh is used directly; when it is empty, the calculator estimates from power and running time.

✓ Daily, monthly, and yearly electricity estimates
✓ Appliance-by-appliance kWh and cost breakdown
✓ Power, time, and measured kWh visible together
✓ Daily contracted-power price and billing-period calculation
✓ Optional fixed monthly electricity charge
✓ Highlights the appliances using the most energy
✓ Blocks letters and invalid values in numeric fields
✓ Saves the current household setup in this browser
✓ No account, analytics, advertising, or telemetry

Private by default

All calculations run on this device. Appliance details and prices stay in the current browser and are never uploaded by the calculator.
