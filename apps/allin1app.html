A lightweight launcher that keeps your desktop clean by bringing apps and shortcuts together in one organized window.

What it does

Everything you launch,
all in one place.

Add apps and shortcuts, organize them into categories, keep favourites close, and clear the clutter from your Windows desktop. Allin1APP is portable and remembers your setup locally.

✓ Add `.exe` apps and `.lnk` shortcuts
✓ Drag, drop, and bulk-import desktop shortcuts
✓ Categories, favourites, custom names, and icons
✓ Light and dark themes
✓ System tray and optional Windows startup
✓ Portable—no installer or Python required

At a glance

Platform
